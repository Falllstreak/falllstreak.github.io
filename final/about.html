Who am I?

Thanks for visiting. I'm Edwin Pacheco, and I'm the creator of this site. I am currently in my first quarter
of pursuing a Software Development degree, which started in the winter of 2023. After taking a break,
I've returned ready to complete my education and pursue a career that I'm passionate about.

My goal in that work is to ensure that people can find exactly what they're intending on the internet.
